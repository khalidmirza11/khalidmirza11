2D Design and Planing

2D design and planning entail creating detailed visual schematics or blueprints to articulate the structure, layout, or design of objects or spaces in a two-dimensional format.

3D Design and Rendering

3D design and rendering involve crafting digital models that simulate lifelike three-dimensional objects or environments, employing specialized software to visualize and bring to life concepts, products, or architectural spaces with depth, texture, and realistic lighting effects.

Animation / Walk-through

Animation or walk-throughs in the context of design typically refer to the process of using sequential images or frames, often in conjunction with 3D models or designs, to create a dynamic visual presentation that simulates movement or navigation through a space, allowing viewers to experience a virtual tour or exploration of a designed environment.

Virtual Staging

Virtual staging is when computers add furniture and decorations to pictures of empty rooms to make them look nicer and more appealing for selling or renting.
